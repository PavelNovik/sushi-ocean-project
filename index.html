rockin' sushi

Since 2001, Sushi Dan has offered a selection of innovative twists to the classics of Japanese cuisine. The delicacies range from a myriad of flavorful appetizers, top-of-the-line sushi and sashimi, and renown " Rockin' Sushi."

Sushi Dan was featured in the Channel 7 News as one of the "Top Restaurants in Studio City." Also, Sushi Dan is known to be a "celebrity hot spot," and has been seen in various magazines.

For lunch, it's a great spot to grab a quick lunch or to just relax and catch up with friends.

For Dinner, it's a trendy, fun, and loud environment to pre game with your friends before a night out. We also play all your favorite sports with our 3 big-screen HDTVs.

UNIQUE CREATIONS

Every dish is a savory, elegant creation by our sushi chefs. All from the freshest ingredients.

In addition to the trendy top-of-the-line sushi and roll menu, Sushi Dan offers a modest selection of kitchen appetizers, salads, grilled entrees, and noodles, to fit your particular palate!

We also have an extensive lunch menu that is reasonably priced. These lunch specials are served from 11am to 3pm Monday - Friday. Each lunch special comes with a miso soup.
